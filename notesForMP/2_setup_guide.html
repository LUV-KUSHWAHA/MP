Step 5 of 10
Install PostGIS Extension
PostGIS adds spatial superpowers to PostgreSQL. Without it, your database can't understand GPS coordinates or answer queries like "find everything within 500m of this point."
ğŸŒ
Why PostGIS? Normal SQL can compare numbers and text, but it has no concept of "distance." PostGIS adds geometry data types (POINT, POLYGON, LINESTRING) and spatial functions like ST_DWithin (within X meters) and ST_MakePoint (create a coordinate point).
1

3. Check "PostGIS 3.4 Bundle"
4. Click Next â†’ it downloads and installs automatically
5. When asked to create a spatial database, say Yes
2
